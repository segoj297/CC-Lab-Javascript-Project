• The brief for Phase 4: Design in a digital medium, or produce an item with digital parts. Still fixated on a mask, I thought of a transparent one with inner LED lights. A clear mask would show the wearer's face, which the LEDs would illuminate. I made a mold shaped from my Mom's face, wireform, clay, and plaster. I vacuum formed a PET sheet over the mold at the Parsons L3 Shop. With no knowledge in electronics, I borrowed an LED rope light. The mask came out wrong and the rope lights looked shoddy. This iteration received the poorest reception from our instructor, but I came away with the lesson that I should think of concepts more broadly.
