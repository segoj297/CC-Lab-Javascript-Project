• The brief for Phase 1: 2D only, no technology. Since the story described a countryside slowly filling up with people, I wanted to translate this with an animatic. First I painted a watercolor of a countryside. Over the painting I laid out a series of acetates covered with humanoid figures. Flipping each acetate fills up the image with humanoid figures. The idea is to depict people as a plague.
