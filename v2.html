• This is the second iteration of my project. The concept is a hazmat suit with smiling clay faces inside. To evoke the feeling of mounting claustrophobia in the story, I designed something to isolate the wearer and surround them with faces. The intent is to unnerve the wearer, and unnerve people with the sight of a hazmat suit -- which means a biohazard in the area. The idea is a riff off the concept that "people are a plague."
